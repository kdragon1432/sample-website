about Homelessness

What is Homelessness?

The definition of homelessness means not having a home. Being homeless usually means that you do not have a place to stay and live on the streets. However, you can be homeless even with a roof over your head.

The Main Causes of Homelessness

- stagnant wages
- unemployment
- lack of affordable housing
- poverty
- lack of mental health and addiction treatment services
- racial inequality
- domestic violence
- family conflict
- systemic failures
