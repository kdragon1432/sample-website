about LGBTQ+

The rainbow flag is the symbol of the LGBTQ community. It is known as the LGBTQ Pride Flag!

What does LGBTQ stand for?

- [L] lesbian
- [G] gay
- [B] bisexual
- [T] transgender
- [Q] queer
It functions as an unbrella term for sexuality and gender identity.
