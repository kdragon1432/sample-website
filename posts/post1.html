What is an IT Professional?

An IT specialist, computer professional, or an IT professional may be:
[1] someone working in the field of information technology
[2] someone who has undergone training or higher learning in tech institute
[3] someone who has proven extensive knowledge in the area of computing

Job Titles in the IT Field

- computer incident responder
- computer repair technician
- computer scientist
- database administrator
- digital forensics analyst
- firewall administrator
- information technology consultant
- intrusion detection system analyst
- network administrator
- programmer (also known as a software engineer)
- system administrator
- systems analyst
- web developer
