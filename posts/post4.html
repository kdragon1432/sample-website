The Causes of Techie Youth

What is Techie Youth?

Techie Youth is a non-profit charity that focuses on helping at-risk youth by providing computer and technology driven training and education to help them grow into financially self-sufficient professsionals.

Who does Techie Youth serve?

- foster kids
- homeless youth
- justice involed teens and formerly incarcerated youth
- pregant or parenting teenagers
- LGBTQ+ youth
- youth with criminal convictions participating in 2nd change programs
- teens with learning challenges
- kids who were victums of abuse/neglect
